    What is iAmbulasya

    iAmbulansya Medical Emergency Response System

    Was a system built by the students of Polytechnic University of the Philippines in fulfilling the requirements for the degree in Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. It was built for a thesis presented to the faculty of College of Computer and Information Sciences at PolytechnicUniversity of the Philippines. The researchers built the system in order to help the city of Marikina on improving the medical emergency response services in vehicular accidents using Dijksra's Algorithm and Dynamic Routing implemented on the system.
